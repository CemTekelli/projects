Ready to Change your life?

Our life is like climbing a mountain, in the visible part of the mountain: our knowledge, experience and behavior are hidden. But there is a large part of the mountain that is unseen: it contains our beliefs, values, norms, and characteristics. This invisible part affects our daily behavior a lot. During the coaching session, we make this invisible part visible. By reflecting on our own mental processes and frame of reference, we better understand our own emotions, motivations, and thoughts. Would you like to discover your 'invisible' behaviors that prevent you? Let's go on a journey together where he becomes the center of his own power. In this journey, you must challenge yourself to form a strong view of yourself. Who am I and what do I want to achieve? By discovering yourself, realize your own strength

About me

Esin Boztepe was born in Ixelles, Belgium. She knew from a young age that she wanted to coach people. For this reason, she received her first undergraduate degree as an outstanding pedagogical coach at Erasmus University College in Brussels. She started her career as an executive coach in 2019. But she also wanted to explore other areas of coaching. Thus, she became a certified Burnout coach in 2020. She contacted MCC Fatih Elibol of ICF in 2020 to obtain a globally recognized certificate. She started training with him to get the title of PCC (Professional Certified Coach). During her training, she received numerous certificates in different themes such as transactional analysis, gestalt therapy, group coaching and mentor coaching. Of course, this is just the beginning because learning is a lifelong process that no one can escape, especially as a coach.
